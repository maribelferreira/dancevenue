Welcome to dance venue!

We are a team of professional teachers who provide people of every age and experience level with affordable quality dance classes in a wide range of styles including: salsa, latin line, zumba, bachata, modern and street dance. Our classes are designed to be motivating, supportive and most importantly, fun. Come join us anytime!

  • No registration.
  • No experience required.
  • No dress code.
  • Student discount.
  • First class is FREE!
