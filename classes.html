Classes

Our range of dance classes are suitable for all levels of fitness.

Our classes

All our classes are run so that a new section of routine is taught every lesson. This way you don't need to attend every class and you wont feel left behind.

With easy-to-follow moves and no pressure to get the steps exactly right, the emphasis is on fun and enjoyment. By integrating dance classes regurlarly into your life you will:

  • Burn calories.
  • Build strenght.
  • Improve flexibility & balance.
  • Reduce stress.
  • Boost confidence.
  • Increase stamina.
  • Socialise.

What to wear & bring

You can wear anything you feel comfortable in, but be prepared to stretch and move! Trainers or comfortable shoes are a must and layers are a good idea to keep you warm as you stretch and cool down. We also recommend to bring a hand towel to dry off with, and plenty of water.
